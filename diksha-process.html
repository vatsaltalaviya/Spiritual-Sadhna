Sacred Tradition

DIKSHA PROCESS

"Online and In-Person Initiation Within the Parampara"

01.

Common Foundation

For all Formats of Diksha

1

Application

Submit the Diksha Application Form with full transparency regarding background and intention.

2

Stage 0

Mandatory preparatory phase to establish daily discipline and lifestyle alignment.

3

Evaluation

Personal assessment of readiness, clarity of intention, and suitability for Sādhana.

4

Confirmation

Diksha is confirmed only when consistency and sincerity are demonstrated.

"There is no automatic initiation."

02.

Online Diksha

Pre-Diksha Preparation

  • • Specific fasting or lifestyle instructions
  • • Clean practice space prepared at home
  • • Required materials arranged in advance

Transmission Session

Conducted via secure platform. Clear mantra articulation and formal initiation moment under structured guidance.

Post-Diksha Protocol

Written instructions provided, daily practice guidelines, and scheduled follow-up check-ins.

"Online Diksha demands the same seriousness as physical presence. Distractions or a casual environment are not acceptable."
03.

In-Person Diksha

Ritual Preparation

  • • Personal Interview & Clarification
  • • Purification procedures & Sankalpa
  • • Structured altar arrangement

Formal Initiation

  • • Mantra transmission & Nyāsa
  • • Instruction in ritual structure
  • • Direct blessing under Guru guidance

Continued Guidance

Observance period, daily structure, and continued reporting/review.

May occur at ashram space, retreats, or designated sacred locations as determined by the Guru.
04.

Which Format is Preferred?

"The Parampara does not privilege distance over sincerity."

Online Diksha is valid when conducted with absolute discipline, containment, and ritual purity within your sacred space.

In-person Diksha may be required for advanced stages involving complex rituals or specific higher transmissions.

The Guru determines appropriateness of format based on readiness.

05.

Responsibility After Diksha

✓

Uninterrupted daily Sādhana

✓

Observe strict confidentiality

✓

Report difficulties honestly

✓

No modification of instruction

"Transmission strengthens through practice."

06.

Important Principles

Activation

Diksha is not a ceremony; it is activation of the lineage current within the disciple.

Readiness

Readiness determines timing. No initiation is conferred for curiosity or prestige.

Guru-āgya

Guru-āgya governs progression. Obedience and humility create receptivity.

Maryādā

Maryādā protects the practice. Conduct and discipline are the boundaries of transmission.

Sacred transmission.

Disciplined continuation.
