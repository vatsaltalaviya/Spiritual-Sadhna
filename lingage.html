Unbroken Protection

THE LINEAGE

"Authentic transmission flowing through realized masters, preserved through discipline, and transmitted through living initiation."

Siddhashram

The Inner Seat of Tapas

Siddhashram is the subtle seat of realised beings whose tapas sustains the current of transmission. It represents the living reservoir of spiritual attainment preserved through Rishi tradition.

To stand within this Parampara is to stand within that current. Transmission does not originate from individual effort alone; it is sustained by the Siddha-field.

Lineage Pillar

Paramhans Swami Sachchidanand Ji

"He embodied renunciation, depth of practice, and uncompromising discipline. He upheld the principle that spiritual transmission must be earned through austerity."

His teachings emphasised the seriousness of Sādhana and the necessity of inner qualification before higher transmissions. Through him, the Siddhashram current flowed into the next generation.

Paramhans Swami Nikhileshwaranand Ji

(Gṛhastha identity: Dr. Narayan Dutt Shrimali)

He united intense Tantric discipline with accessibility to sincere seekers. His life demonstrated that profound spiritual attainment can manifest within worldly engagement without dilution of tapas.

He repeatedly affirmed that the Guru is not a personality but a Tattva — a living principle of awakening. His departure in 1998 did not conclude the current; the Guru-Mandal remains operative.

HE TRANSMITTED:

  • ● Mantra–Tantra–Yantra Sādhana
  • ● Śrī Vidyā Krāmik Dīkṣā
  • ● Mahāvidyā Sādhana
  • ● Guru–Śiṣya Discipline

Continuity Through Brahmanshi

Brahmanshi stands within this lineage as a transmitter under Guru-ādeśa. Her work is not independent invention; it is continuation.

✦ Maryādā of secrecy
✦ Progressive Krāmik Dīkṣā
